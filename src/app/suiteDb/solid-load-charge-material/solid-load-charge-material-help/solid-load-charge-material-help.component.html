Latent heat of fusion

The amount of thermal energy required to convert a unit mass of the solid material into liquid without change in temperature.

Melting Point

The temperature at which a given solid will melt.

Specific heat of liquid from molten material

Energy required to change a unit mass of the material by unit temperature.

Average specific heat of the solid material

Required average heat when material per unit mass is raised by unit temperature.
