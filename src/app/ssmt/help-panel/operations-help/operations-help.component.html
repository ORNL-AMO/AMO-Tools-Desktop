Steam Operations Help
Enter measured data to calculate your system's annual savings potential.
Operating Hours
Denotes the annual operating hours during which the facility utilizes the steam system. Either the total hours of operation or the operating schedule (day/week, hours/day) should be entered.
Site Import Power
Average power imported (positive) or exported (negative). Does not include power generated and used onsite.
Make-up Water Temperature
Average temperature of makeup water. Generally close to average outside temperature.
Fuel Cost
Cost of fuel per energy unit.
Electricity Cost
Average cost per unit of electricity.
Make-up Water Cost
Cost of makeup water per unit volume.
