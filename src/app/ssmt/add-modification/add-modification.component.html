The Modify All Conditions section is an expert view, allowing you to change any input. You can create many different scenarios, to compare changes to your system. Notes for each loss page can be added in the right tab (NOTES), these will be added to your final report. Data will be copied from your current baseline condition.

The Explore Opportunities section is a novice view to help you find and evaluate different opportunities for your system. Notes can be added in the right tab (NOTES), these will be added to your final report. Data will be copied from your current baseline condition.
