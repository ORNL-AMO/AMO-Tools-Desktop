Annotations
Click the chart to select the data you want to annotate
The annotation feature may cause slow graph performance on some large datasets. By default, annotations are turned off for these datasets. MEASUR considers a large dataset to be greater than 200,000 datapoints, or roughly 7 days of log data at 3 second intervals.
Large Dataset Tips
  • Existing annotations will remain visible and will not affect performance.
  • Narrowing the range of the graph by clicking and dragging an area before turning on annotations will increase graph speed and usability
  • Annotations can be turned off/on at any time in the sidebar options.
