Feedback

We are always looking to improve MEASUR and get feedback on accuracy, usability, presentation or new features. Please take our survey on Survey Monkey: https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/DOE-AMO-TOOLS. Thank you for your feedback!

Bug Reporting

To report a bug or otherwise provide feedback, please email Gina Accawi at accawigk@ornl.gov. Please include your operating system (e.g., Windows 10, Mac OS 10.12), a screenshot of the issue and download your current data set so that we can understand the problem.

Click to download your current data set. The current data set download provides us with all the data on the tool, but no information outside of the tool.

You are using an international version of the application, please specify that when reporting bugs.

Thank you for your feedback!
