Help

Operating Hours
Denotes the annual operating hours for the facility. Either the total hours of operation or the operating schedule (day/week, hours/day) should be entered.
Assessment Settings
Your system basics help define the units of measure and other information related to the system you are modeling for this assessment. These settings are inherited by default from your directory or applications settings and can be customized for this assessment.
Operation Costs
Enter your current consumption and costs per utility type, for the facility over an entire year.
Compressed air and steam fields are only to be used if the facility receives compressed air or steam as a utility. If they are generated at the facility, then they should be considered part of other utilities (electricity or fuel).
Total Emission Output Rate
The total emissions per unit of energy use. By default, this is set by the dropdown options, but is changeable. The default values are only kg of carbon dioxide (not carbon dioxide equivalent).
Fuel Type
Specific fuel creating the emission (set by Energy Source).
Energy Source
Type of fuel creating the emission (used to set Fuel Type dropdown options). Can choose from Steam & Hot Water, Natural Gas, Petroleum-based fuels (including diesel and gasoline), biomass fuels (including biodiesel and landfill gas), coal, and other fuels (including coke).
