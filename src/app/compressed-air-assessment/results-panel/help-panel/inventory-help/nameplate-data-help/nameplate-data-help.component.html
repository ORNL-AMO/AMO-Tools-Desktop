Compressor Type
A general description of the air compressor design and style. The compressor type options in MEASUR are: single stage lubricant-injected rotary screw, two stage lubricant-injected rotary screw, two stage lubricant-free rotary screw, single stage reciprocating, two stage reciprocating, and centrifugal.
