Full Load Discharge Pressure (cut-in)
The pressure at which the air compressor begins to deliver air; at this point, the compressor may reload or turn on, and is delivering full flow.
Max Full Load Discharge Pressure (cut-out)
The pressure at which the air compressor cuts out or unloads, in pounds per square inch gauge. May require additional power. At this point, the compressor may unload, be fully modulated, or turn off.
