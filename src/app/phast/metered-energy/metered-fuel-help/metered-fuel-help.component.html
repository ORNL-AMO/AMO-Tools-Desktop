Metered Energy Help
Enter data about the energy of processing heating equipment based on metered fuel use. This allows the user to compare PHAST calculations to the meter data of the equipment. This method calculates total energy used by a furnace by multiplying metered fuel energy used by the measurement period (hours/days/weeks).
Fuel Type
Fuel used in heat zone.
Flow Rate
Metered flow rate of fuel, as measured during the collection period. If flow rate is unknown, it can be calculated using the button below using data about.
Heating Value - HHV
Higher heating value of the fuel (calculated based on fuel composition).
Fuel Collection Time
Time period over which fuel meter data was collected.
Electricity Used
Metered electricity used by auxiliary equipment during collection period.
Electricity Collection Time
Time period over which auxiliary electricity meter data was collected.
Type of Fuel
Gas or Solid/Liquid Fuels.
Metered Fuel Energy
The amount of fuel energy measured by the fuel meter during the data collection time
Calculate using Flow Rate
The volume of fuel measured by the meter during the data collection time
Flow Rate During Collection
Metered flow rate of fuel, as measured during the collection period.
