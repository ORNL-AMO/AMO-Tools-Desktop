System Basics

Your system basics help define the units of measure and other information related to the system you are modeling for this assessment. These settings are inherited by default from your directory or applications settings and can be customized for this assessment.

Operating Costs

Per unit cost of fuel, steam and electricity related to the operation of furnace.

Operating Hours

Denotes the annual operating hours during which the facility utilizes furnace loads. Either the total hours of operation or the operating schedule (day/week, hours/day) should be entered.

Use the notes section to add a note about the operating conditions at the time of the assessment.
