System Basics
Your system basics help define the units of measure and other information related to the system you are modeling for this assessment. These settings are inherited by default from your directory or applications settings and can be customized for this assessment.
Note: The words furnace, process heating equipment, process heating system, and PH System may be used interchangeably throughout this tool.
Operating Costs
Per unit cost of fuel, steam and electricity related to the operation of the process heating system.
Operating Hours
Denotes the annual operating hours during which the facility utilizes process heating equipment to heat or melt the charge material. Either the total hours of operation or the operating schedule (day/week, hours/day) should be entered.
Use the Operating Conditions At Time of Assessment box to add notes about the operating conditions at the time of the assessment.
