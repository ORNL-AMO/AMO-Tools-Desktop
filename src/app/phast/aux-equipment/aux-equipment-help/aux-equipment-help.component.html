Auxiliary Equipment Help

Enter data for additional/auxiliary equipment that uses energy in connection with process heating equipment.

Equipment Name
Description:

Enter a descriptive name for the equipment (ex. Fan #1)

Duty Cycle
Description:

% of time equipment is on

Motor Power
Description:

Calculate motor power based on phase, voltage, current and power factor or enter rated power.

Motor Phase
Description:

1 or 3 phases

Supply Voltage
Description:

Electric power supply (volts) into the motor

Units: V
Minimum Value: 0
Maximum Value: 480

Average Current
Description:

Average current supplied to the motor

Power Factor
Description:

Average power factor incoming to the motor

Total Connected Power
Description:

Power used by the equipment

Rated Capacity
Description:

The percent of rated capacity that is used.
