Slag Losses Help

Weight

Weight of material discharged. Note that the values vary with time during the cycle and this is average over the cycle time.

Units: Lb/hr or kg/hr
Minimum Value: 0

Inlet Temperature

Inlet temperature for the material discharged.

Units: ℉ or ℃

Outlet Temperature

Outlet temperature for the material discharged.

Units: ℉ or ℃

Specific Heat

Specific Heat of the slag material.

Units: ℉ or ℃
Minimum Value: 0

Correction Factor

Engineering correction factor multiplied to the result to account for a known systematic error.

Units: Btu/(SCF-℉) or kJ/(kg-℃)
Minimum Value: 0

Savings Suggestions
  • Improve material handling with the furnace to increase yield and reduce slag.
  • Install combustion gas (air) pre-heater.
