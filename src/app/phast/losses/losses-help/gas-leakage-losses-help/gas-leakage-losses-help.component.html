Note: These energy saving measures are for guidance only. Not all measures are applicable under all operating conditions. There may additional measures when considering specific situations and the user is encouraged to review and apply the appropriate measures
Furnace Draft Pressure
Furnace pressure measured at the level of the opening. It is measured by using an inclined tube manometer or a digital gage. Units of measurement is in terms of inch water column. Hot gases leak out when the pressure is positive.
Opening Area
This is value of open area from where hot gases are leaking out.

Temperature of Gases Leaking
Temperature of gases leaking out of the opening. In most cases it is safe and accurate enough to measure and use furnace (or zone) temperature for this value.

Specific density of gases at flue exit measured relative to air at standard temperature and pressure (0.0763 lb/scf).
