Modify Operation Conditions Help

Operating Hours

Enter the typical operating schedule of the plant. If the annual operating hours is known enter that value directly in the last row.

Operating Costs

Enter the per unit cost associated with fuel, steam and electricity. This is usually specified by the utility provider. For calculating the operating costs of self-generated fuel, enter the total cost associated with fuel production and divide by the gross fuel consumption.

Implementation Cost

Cost associated with executing the modifications to the baseline system.

Savings Suggestions
  • Enter data about Operations Settings (operating hours and costs) and the cost of implementing a modification.
