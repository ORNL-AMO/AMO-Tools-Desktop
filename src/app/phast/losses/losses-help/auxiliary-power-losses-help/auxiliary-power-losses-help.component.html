Use variable speed drive where possible
  • Improve power factor
  • Reduce motor load through assessment of use of utilities (water, compressed air, hydraulics etc.) to eliminate waste and reduce usage where possible
  • Consider alternate drive system where possible
  • Use fuel-air ratio control system to maintain desired (preferably 10% excess air) for the range of burner turndown
  • Replace excess air burners by ratio type burners. Consider process requirements (i.e. make up air) to evaluate benefits of ratio type burners
  • Replace atmospheric or even premix burners where possible to use nozzle mix burners with high turn down
