Surge airflow at design conditions
The air flow at the surge point, aka the blow-off point
Max. full load (surge) pressure
For Centrifugal compressors, this the preassure at max load, aka surge pressure
Capacity at max full load pressure
Full load airflow for a centrifugal compressor when operating the compressor at its maximum stable discharge pressure.
Min. full load (stonewall) pressure
For Centrifugal compressors, this the preassure at min load, aka stonewall pressure
Capacity at min. full load pressure
Maximum airflow delivery capability of a centrifugal compressor. Reduction of discharge pressure will not increase airflow since the air has reached sonic speed.
