Discharge Pressure
The maximum pressure attainable at full flow (measured in pounds per square inch gauge); usually the unload pressure setting for load/unload control or the maximum pressure attainable before capacity control begins. May require additional power.
Air Flow
The airflow at the maximum full-load discharge pressure, in actual cubic feet per minute.
Power
The power at which the compressor reaches full flow, may require additional power above full load.
Discharge Pressure
The pressure at which the air compressor cuts out or unloads, in pounds per square inch gauge. May require additional power. At this point, the compressor may unload, be fully modulated, or turn off.
Air Flow
The airflow at the maximum full-load discharge pressure, in actual cubic feet per minute.
Power
The power at which the air compressor cuts out or unloads. May require additional power above full load. At this point, the compressor may unload, be fully modulated, or turn off.
