Water Reduction
This calculator is used to quantify the energy savings associated with reducing water/wastewater usage.
This calculator can be used to quantify typical water/wastewater energy savings opportunities like:
Reducing the operation time of equipment which uses water
Install timers to automatically shut off water flow when not required
Recycle water from other parts/processes of the facility
Reduce use of once through cooling
Substitute water where possible (air cooling, alternative closed-loop coolants, waterless processes)
Check and replace steam traps regularly
Water Type

Water Cost

Method of Measurement
Choose the method to be used to determine water consumption/wastewater disposal.
Volume Meter - The best way to measure water consumption/wastewater disposal is with a totalizing flow meter (measureing total water volume to pass).
Metered Flow - The best way to measure water consumption/wastewater disposal is with a totalizing flow meter (measuring flow rate).
Bucket Method - The second most accurate means of measuring water consumption/waste disposal is done by counting the number of seconds it takes to fill a bucket of known volume.
