Fan System Optimization Checklist Help
For fans over 50 hp that operate more than 4000 hours per year
Use this checklist to qualitatively select the top optimization projects for FSAT analysis. Make a copy of this list for each of your major systems, then go through the list and add up the points for the conditions that apply. ood optimization opportunity. A total score of four or more points probably indicates a very good opportunity.
Note: Fans with adjustable speed drives usually are not good candidates for optimization.
Operating Hours
Number of hours per year the lighting is on. This is not necessarily how many hours the facility is operating. Be sure to include normally scheduled overtime.
