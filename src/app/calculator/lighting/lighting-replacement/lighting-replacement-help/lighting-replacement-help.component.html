Lighting Replacement
This calculator can be used to calculate the energy savings of typical energy saving opportunities:
Reduce the operation time of lighting equipment.
Convert to a lower power use fixture.
Reduce the number of lighting fixtures.
Turn off the lights when people are not present.
Hours per Day

Days per Month
Number of days per month the lighting is on. This is not necessarily how many days the facility is operating. Be sure to include normally scheduled overtime.
