Compressed Air Pressure Reduction
This calculator can be used to quantify the electricity saved from reducing compressed air system pressure. The benefits of lowering system pressure are twofold:
  • Reducing the pressure lowers the work done by the compressor and hence lesser electricity is drawn.
  • Reducing the pressure also lowers the amount of air delivered to plant and thus cuts down on the air lost in the form of artificial demand and air leaks.
This calculator only calculates the electricity saved at the compressors and hence its savings are not relevant when compressed air is brought from outside.
Note: This calculator is not valid for centrifugal compressors
