Compressor Cycle
******** Ask Alex or Kristia ***********
Load Time
Time that the compressor is loaded during a cycle.
Unload Time
Time the compressor is unloaded during a cycle, the time allowed for pressure drop.
Compressor Capacity
The rated capacity of the compressor.
Full Load or cut-in pressure
The lower limit for the pressure band, the final reciever pressure before the compressor loads.
Unload or cut-out pressure
The upper limit for the pressure band, the initial pressure of the reciever when the compressor unloads.
