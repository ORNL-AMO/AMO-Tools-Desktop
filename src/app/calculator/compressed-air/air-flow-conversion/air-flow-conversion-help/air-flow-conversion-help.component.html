Actual to Standard Airflow Help
The calculator converts acfm (Actual cubic feet per minute) to scfm (Standard cubic feet per minute) and vice versa for the given conditions using either ASME standard conditions or CAGI/ISO standard conditions.
Standard Conditions
Standard conditions (STP) are standard sets of conditions for experimental measurements to be established to allow comparisons to be made between different sets of data. These vary between different official standards. Conditions with temperatures greater than 32F are often called “Normal Conditions”. For converting airflow, the CAGI and ASME are the most used in the U.S.
