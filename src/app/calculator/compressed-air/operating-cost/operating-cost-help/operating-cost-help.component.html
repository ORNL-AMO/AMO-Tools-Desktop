Operating Costs Help
This calculator helps in finding the cost of operation of the compressor in both fully and partially loaded instances.
Air Flow
Volumetric flow rate of the air in the compressor system, referenced to the compressor inlet conditions.
Compressor Motor Brake Horse Power
Input power required at the compressor input shaft for a specific speed, capacity and pressure condition. Generally shown on the compressor panel.
Compressor Motor Brake Horse Power Un-loaded
Brake horse power in the un-loaded condition.
Annual Operating Hours
Time for which the compressor operates in a year.
Run-time Un-loaded %
Percentage of time the compressor runs in un-loaded condition.
Run-time Loaded %
Percentage of time the compressor runs in loaded condition.
Efficiency Loaded
Percentage of efficiency in loaded condition.
Efficiency Un-loaded
Percentage of efficiency in un-loaded condition.
Cost of Electricity
Cost of electricity per kWh.
