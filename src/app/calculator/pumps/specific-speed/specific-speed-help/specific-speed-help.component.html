Specific Speed Help
For each pump type there is an optimal specific speed based on size, flow rate, and specific speed. Operation at the optimal specific speed allows a pump to operate at maximum efficiency. Hydraulic Institute standards also include factors for wear and surface finish on wetted parts that are not considered in these calculations.
Pump Type
Represents what style of pump is being used based off of the listings in the standard ANSI/HI 1.3-2000.
Pump Speed
The speed of the pump in revolutions per minute (rpm).
Flow Rate
Volume of liquid that travels through the pump in a given time (measured in gallons per minute or gpm).
Head
Head is the height at which a pump can raise water up.
