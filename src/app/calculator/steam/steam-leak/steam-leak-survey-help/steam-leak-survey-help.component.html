Steam - Leak Survey Help
This calculator is used to quantify the energy savings associated with reducing steam leaks.
Steam is a costly utility — even small leaks result in significant losses of energy, water, and boiler chemicals over the course of a year.
Steam leaks reduce system pressure, which degrades the performance and reliability of downstream steam-using equipment.
Every pound of steam lost to a leak must be replaced by the boiler, increasing fuel consumption and accelerating boiler wear.
