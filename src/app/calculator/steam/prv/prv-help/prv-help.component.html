Temperature Value
The temperature at the inlet. If below boiling point, inlet will be a liquid.
Specific Enthalpy Value
Specific Enthalpy (h) is the combination of the steam's internal energy and the work needed to make room for the material, compared to the standard state, in terms of mass.
Specific Entropy Value
Specific Entropy (s) is a measure of the amount of energy which is unavailable to do work, compared to the standard state, in terms of mass.
Saturated Quality Value
Quality is the vapor mass percentage of the steam/water mixture. Quality is zero for liquid water and 1 for steam; a value between 0 and 1 indicates mixed flow (both liquid and vapor).
