Pressure

Absolute Pressure of the steam.

Units: kPa or psi

Quantity Type

To determine steam and liquid water properties, a second state variable, in addition to pressure is required.

Temperature

Temperature of the steam.

Units: C or F

Specific Enthalpy

Specific Enthalpy (h) is the combination of the steam's internal energy and the work needed to make room for the material, compared to the standard state, in terms of mass.

Units: kJ/kg or Btu/lb

Specific Entropy

Specific Entropy (s) is a measure of the amount of energy which is unavailable to do work, compared to the standard state, in terms of mass.

Units: kJ/kgK or Btu/lbF

Quality

Quality is the vapor mass percentage of the steam/water mixture. Quality is zero for liquid water and 1 for steam.
