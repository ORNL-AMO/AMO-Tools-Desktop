Pipe Insulation Reduction
This calculator is used to quantify the energy savings associated with insulating hot pipes.
Disclaimer: Cost savings estimates are based on a simplified system analysis. For steam systems with back-pressure turbines, this analysis may not be accurate.
Operation Hours
Number of annual operating hours for the equipment.
Utility Cost
Per unit cost of natural gas/other in ($/MMBtu). ($/GJ).
System Efficiency
System Efficiency of Natural Gas (or other fuel) system.
Pipe Length
Provide the length of the pipe in (ft) (m).
Pipe Diameter
Select pipe diameter from Nominal Pipe Size list. These options will automatically provide a corresponding value for pipe thickness, this assumes a standard thickness for the pipe diameter.
Average Wind Speed
Enter the average velocity of air flow over the length of the pipe.
Pipe Temperature
Provide the average temperature of the pipe material in (℉) (℃).
Ambient Temperature
Enter the average temperature of the ambient air in (℉) (℃).
Pipe Base Material
Select the base material of the pipe. This will provide a corresponding emissivity to the calculation.
Insulation Material
Select the insulation material, if any, that is used on the pipe.
Insulation Thickness
Provide the thickness of the insulation in (in) (m).
Pipe Jacket Material
Select the pipe jacket material if the pipe has a jacket. A corresponding emissivity will be provided to the calculation.
