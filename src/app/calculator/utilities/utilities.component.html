General Calculators

Pre-assessment / Screening Calculator

This calculator allows the user to enter either energy use information gathered from meters or design specifications (for fuel, electrotechnology or steam furnaces) to calculate energy use for multiple furnaces. This allows users to compare the relative energy use of furnaces within a facility to prioritize energy evaluations and efficiency improvements.

Unit Converter

Use the unit converter for quick unit conversion calculations.

Combined Heat and Power

Use this calculator to conduct a preliminary screening of the potential cost savings from CHP based on annual electricity consumption, annual thermal loads, average electricity and fuel prices, and annual operating hours.

Cash Flow Diagram

The Cash flow diagram displays the revenues (savings) and costs associated with an investment.

Power Factor Correction

This calculator is used to identify the capacitance (in kVAR) required for improving the power factor to the proposed level.

CO2 Savings Calculator

This calculator lets the user determine carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions for given electricity and fuel uses.

Electricity Reduction

This calculator is used to quantify the energy savings associated with reducing electricity usage.

Natural Gas Reduction

This calculator is used to quantify the energy savings associated with reducing natural gas usage.
