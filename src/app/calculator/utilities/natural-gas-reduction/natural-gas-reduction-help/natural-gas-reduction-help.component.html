Natural Gas Reduction Help
This calculator is used to quantify the energy savings associated with reducing natural gas usage.
This calculator can be used to quantify typical natural gas energy savings opportunities like
Reduce the operation time of equipment which uses natural gas
Decrease the flow rate of the "fluid" being heated. (The most common "fluids" heated are water and air)
Turn off equipment that uses natural gas
Change set point to a lower temperature

Number of days per month the equipment is on. Be sure to include normally scheduled overtime.
Months per Year
Numbers of months per year.
Fuel Cost
Per unit cost of natural gas in ($/{{settings.energyResultUnit}}).
System Efficiency
Specify the natural gas system efficiency.
Method of Measurement
Choose the method to be used to determine natural gas consumption.
Flow Meter Method - The best way to measure natural gas consumption is with a flow meter. Pay attention to the units in the meter before entering data.
Mass Flow Method for Air - This method uses the mass flow of air heated by the natural gas to back-calculate the natural gas consumption. The mass flow of air can be determined either from a mass flow meter or using nameplate data. Using mass flow meter readings is much more accurate than the nameplate readings. Mass flow calculations use ASHRAE accepted formulae to calculate consumption.
Mass Flow Method for Water - This method uses the mass flow of water heated by the natural gas to back-calculate the natural gas consumption. The mass flow of water can be determined either from a mass flow meter or using nameplate data.
Other Method - Choose this option if you are using an off sheet method to determine the natural gas consumption.
Fuel Flow Rate

Number of Units
The number of identical units in the specified system. When units of different size have been combined previously, enter 1.
Air Flow Measurement
Air flow may be either measured or taken from the equipment nameplate. (Measured data is more accurate)
Area of Duct
The area of the duct where air heated by the natural gas is flowing.
Air Velocity
The velocity of air heated by natural gas measured by a mass flow meter.
Air Flow Rate
Air flow rate as given by the equipment nameplate.

Outlet Temperature

Water Flow Rate

Inlet Temperature
Input the average temperature of the incoming water.
Outlet Temperature
Input the average temperature of the discharge water.
