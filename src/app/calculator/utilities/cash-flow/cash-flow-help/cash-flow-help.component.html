Lifetime

Expected Lifetime of the equipment/modification (years)

Units: yr

Annual Energy Savings

Annual expected savings resulting from the new process modification equipment (if the modification includes any savings beyond energy, could also include this here)

Units: $/yr
Minimum Value Allowed: 0

Salvage

The estimated value that the owner is paid when the item is sold at the end of its useful life.

Units: $
Minimum Value Allowed: 0

Installation Cost

The expected cost to purchase and install the new process modification or equipment.

Units: $
Minimum Value Allowed: 0

Annual Operation Cost

Annual Operation costs (including maintenance and other yearly costs) for the equipment or increase in operation costs with the new equipment (excluding fuel). (If there is an operation cost savings, that should be include in “Annual Energy Savings”).

Units: $/yr
Minimum Value Allowed: 0

Annual Fuel Cost

Annual Fuel costs for the equipment or increase in fuel costs with the new equipment. (If there is a fuel savings, that should be include in “Annual Energy Savings”)

Units: $/yr
Minimum Value Allowed: 0

Disposal

The estimated cost to dispose of the modification or equipment at the end of its useful life.

Units: $
Minimum Value Allowed: 0
