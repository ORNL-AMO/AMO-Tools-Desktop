Operations Help
Enter data related to the operations
Annual Operating Hours
Enter the total number of hours the system operates per year. This value is used for energy and cost calculations.
Electricity Cost
Enter the cost of electricity per kWh. This is used to calculate the operating cost of the system.
Fuel Cost
Enter the cost of fuel. This is used for systems that use fuel for heating or other processes.
Do chiller load schedules vary?
Select the appropriate option to determine if chiller load schedules vary. This question is relevant for multi-chiller systems that operate for extended periods with unequal chiller loading. Select 'No' if the system employs a single chiller
Chilled Water Supply Temperature
Enter the temperature of the chilled water supplied to the system. The allowable value ranges from 35 to 55 degrees Fahrenheit.
Condenser Cooling Method
Chillers require cooling of the chiller condenser as part of the refrigeration cycle. The condenser is typically cooled by a fan-driven air stream (Air-Cooled) or water flowing through a heat exchanger (Water-Cooled). All chillers in the system are assumed to use the same condenser cooling method. Select the method of cooling from the pull-down menu.
