Estimate Outlet Pressure
This calculator is to estimate outlet pressure when outlet pressure cannot be directly measured, by estimating potential pressure losses. Losses can be estimated using information from fan/accessory manufacturers or by consulting references such as AMCA Publication 201 â€“ Fans and Systems.
Fan Outlet System Effect Equivalent Loss
Loss in pressure due to system effects. Duct turns at the outlet of the fan result in adverse aerodynamics, referred to as system effects (causing non-uniform loading of fan impeller, swirling flow, inability to develop a uniform flow profile on discharge). To minimize system effect loss, minimize the number of curves in the outlet ductwork.
Outlet Damper Loss
Pressure losses due to dampers at the outlet of the fan.
Outlet Ductwork Loss
Pressure losses resulting from outlet ductwork.
Air Treatment Loss
Pressure loss due to air treatment at the outlet, usually for air quality.
System Damper Loss
Pressure losses due to dampers in the system downstream of the fan, such as a backdraft damper.
Process Requirements (Discharge Side - Fixed)
Pressure losses due to system components downstream of the fan, such as hoods, louvers, diffusers.
Process Requirements (Discharge Side - Variable)
Pressure losses due to system components downstream of the fan, such as hoods, louvers, diffusers.
